Salad Shirazi

Description

It is a salad originating from Shiraz. It is a common meal accompaniment

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 4 cucumbers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp dried mint
  • 1 lime
  • Sour grape juice
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the onion, cucumbers and tomatoes. Peel the onion and chop them finely in small cubes. Do the same for cucumbers and tomatoes. Then mix them.
  2. Add the sour grape or lime juice to the salad. Stir the content.
  3. Smash some dried mint and sprinkle them on the salad. Add some salt and pepper and taste it depending on your preferred taste. Finally squeeze a fresh lime on the salad to enhance the flavor.
